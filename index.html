Dental Exams

Routine Dental Exams and Check ups at 30$.

"We believe that routine dental exams and check-ups are essential for maintaining optimal oral health. That's why we offer comprehensive exams and check-ups at an affordable price of just $30.

During your appointment, our experienced dental professionals will thoroughly examine your teeth and gums, checking for any signs of decay, disease, or other issues. We will also perform a professional cleaning to remove any plaque or tartar buildup that may have accumulated since your last visit.

What we do

Teeth Whitening

Achieving a brighter, more confident smile has never been easier with our professional teeth whitening services. Our safe and effective treatments can remove stubborn stains and discoloration caused by aging, smoking, or certain foods and beverages. We offer both in-office and take-home whitening options to fit your schedule and budget

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a popular and effective way to replace missing teeth and restore your smile. Our team of dental professionals has years of experience in dental implant placement, ensuring that your procedure is safe, comfortable, and successful. We use the latest technology and materials to ensure the best possible outcome

Invisalign

Straighten your teeth without the hassle of traditional braces with Invisalign. Our clear aligner treatment is virtually invisible and can provide effective results in a fraction of the time of traditional braces. Our team of Invisalign specialists will work with you to create a personalized treatment plan that fits your unique needs

Root Canal Therapy

If you are experiencing severe tooth pain or sensitivity, you may need root canal therapy. Our experienced endodontists can perform this procedure with the utmost care and precision, using the latest technology to ensure a comfortable and successful outcome

Pediatric Dentistry

We believe that establishing good oral health habits early in life is key to a lifetime of healthy teeth and gums. Our pediatric dentistry services provide a safe and welcoming environment for children of all ages, where they can receive the specialized care they need to maintain optimal oral health

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies can happen at any time, which is why we offer 24/7 emergency dental care. Whether you have a broken tooth, severe pain, or another urgent dental issue, our team of dental professionals is here to provide immediate relief and expert care.
